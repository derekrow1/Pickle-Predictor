Overview

This dashboard (“App”) is used internally for Josh’s Pickles to analyze business data from connected services such as QuickBooks Online and Shopify.

Data we access

When you authorize a connection, the App may access data from your connected account, such as company info, accounting transactions, orders, customers, and related records, depending on the permissions you grant.

How we use data

  • To display analytics and dashboards
  • To compute financial and operational metrics
  • To support scenario modeling and reporting

How we store data

Access tokens and credentials are stored as encrypted environment variables on our hosting provider and are not shared publicly. We do not sell personal information.

Sharing

Data is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the App (e.g. hosting) or as required by law.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data, including HTTPS and restricted access. No system is 100% secure.

For questions, contact the App administrator.
